GPA & CGPA — How this calculator works

GPA is a weighted average for one semester. Each grade maps to points: O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7, B=6, C=5, U=0. For each subject, we compute GradePoint × Credit, sum them, and divide by the sum of credits counted (only rows where you selected a grade; U counts as 0 but its credits stay in). CGPA combines your previous semester (GPA×Credits) with the current semester’s exact total grade points, then divides by total credits.
